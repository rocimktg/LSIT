The Five Tenets of Taekwondo

예의

Courtesy

Ye-wee

A bow of respect to all who enter; humility opens every door.

염치

Integrity

Yom-chee

Unwavering truth—
clear as a still mountain lake, strong as bedrock.

인내

Perseverance

Een-nay

Endless renewal—
the river carves stone; the student rises again.

극기

Self Control

Gook-gee

Perfect balance—
the still point within motion, breath finding center.

백절불굴

Indomitable Spirit

Baekjool Boolgool

Unbreakable resolve—
fire that stands, gives all, and endures.
